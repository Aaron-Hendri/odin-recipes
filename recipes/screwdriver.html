Screwdriver

This fun and simple drink is perfect for any occasion,
whether it's a relaxing evening or a night of fun
the screwdrive is sure to turn you just right!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 ounces vodka
  • Orange juice, freshly squeezed, to top

Directions

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice, then add the vodka.
  2. Top with the orange juice.
